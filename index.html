About

About Me

Lit & Love, LLC was founded by award-winning educator Comfort Agboola with a clear purpose: to strengthen literacy instruction, elevate student voice, and support educators in building learning environments where students thrive.

With over a decade of experience in the classroom and instructional leadership, Comfort brings a deep understanding of what teachers and students truly need to succeed. Literacy is more than reading and writing — it is confidence, agency, and access to opportunity.

"Lit & Love is grounded in collaboration, intentional design, and practical application. Every partnership is tailored to meet the unique needs of each school community."

— Comfort Agboola, Founder
