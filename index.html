Lit & Love, LLC was founded by award-winning educator Comfort Agboola with a clear purpose: to strengthen literacy instruction, elevate student voice, and support educators in building learning environments where students thrive.

With over a decade of experience in the classroom and instructional leadership, Comfort brings a deep understanding of what teachers and students truly need to succeed. Her work is rooted in the belief that literacy is more than reading and writing — it is confidence, agency, and access to opportunity.

Core Beliefs
• Literacy boosts confidence, voice, and opportunities.
• Teachers need meaningful support and clear tools.
• Students excel when they feel seen, valued, and challenged.
• Sustainable change requires intentional systems, not quick fixes.
• Education should balance rigor with compassion.
Services Offered
• Literacy-focused professional development
• Instructional coaching and curriculum design
• Writing workshops and literacy enrichment
• Research-based classroom strategies
• Support for student-centered learning environments
• Individual tutoring and academic support for families

"Lit & Love is grounded in collaboration, intentional design, and practical application. Every partnership is tailored to meet the unique needs of each school community, with a focus on strengthening instruction, supporting educators, and helping students grow with confidence."

— Comfort Agboola, Founder
